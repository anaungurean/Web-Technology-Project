Frequently Asked Questions

Locked out of your HeMa account?

1.Open the HeMa app

2.On the main view of the app, tap "Sign in"

3.Tap "Forgot password?"

4.Enter the email address associated with your account

5.Tap "Send" and you will receive an email with a link to reset your password (make sure you check the junk or spam folder)

Here's how to add photos/images of your plants:

1.Tap the "Add plants to my garden" button from the Home Page

2.Tap the "Add photo" button in the lower right corner of the main page

3.Select an image from your photo library

4."Save"

Here's how to search for a specific plant:

1.Tap the "Find plants" button from the Home Page

2.You will be redirected to the page all the plants where there is a filter section

3.Tick the features you want

4."Filter"

You don't know how to find out information about the plant?

1.Tap the plant button for which you want to find out its location

2.You will be redirected to the plant's profile page

3.The location should appear at "Hashtags" or "Place of collections"
